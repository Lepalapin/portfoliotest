About

For our year-end project, we were a group of 6. We had 8 weeks to make a functionnal game. For now, the project is the result of these 8 weeks of work, but we're planning to continue this game further.

Pitch

"Star Colosseum", this space arena proposes to diffuse throughout the galaxy its gladiator fights. The Harvesters are tasked with capturing new warriors all over space, and this time they have come upon you! Face the champions of the infernal colosseum and prove to them that they were wrong by capturing you!
